Terms & Conditions

Please read the following before creating your account. It describes what we collect, how we use it, and where it lives.

1. Information we collect

  • From Google Sign-In: your Google account ID, email address, name, and profile picture.
  • From this form: your first name, last name, WhatsApp number, and referral source.
  • From your subscription activity: the plan you select, the UPI transaction reference (UTR) you submit, your billing start and renewal dates, and — for first-time subscribers only — the Google Meet link assigned to your onboarding call.
  • Automatic: the timestamp of your most recent sign-in.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and maintain your account and subscription record.
  • To deliver subscription notifications to your WhatsApp number: payment confirmations, the Google Meet link for setup, renewal reminders, and plan-change acknowledgements.
  • To display your account details, plan, and billing dates on your dashboard.
  • To let our support team identify you when you contact us.

We do not send promotional or marketing messages. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

3. Where your information is stored

  • Application backend: a Node.js server hosted on Render.
  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL), with row-level security enforced so only our authenticated backend can read or write your record.
  • All connections between your browser, our backend, and the database use HTTPS/TLS.

4. Third-party service providers

We share the minimum data necessary with the following providers, solely to operate the service:

  • Google — to authenticate your sign-in (email and name only).
  • Supabase — as our database host.
  • Render — as our application host.
  • WhatsApp — to deliver subscription messages to the number you provide.
  • Slack — internal notifications to our operations team (your name, email, and plan events) so we can process your subscription promptly.

5. Payments

  • Payments are made directly to our UPI ID devtoolpro@ybl. We do not collect or store card numbers, bank credentials, CVV, or UPI PIN.
  • We store the UTR / transaction reference you enter so we can verify and correlate your payment with your account.

6. Cancellation, deletion, and updates

  • You can cancel your subscription any time from the dashboard. Access continues until the end of the current billing cycle.
  • To request account deletion, message us on WhatsApp. Your record is moved out of the active list; payment references are retained for audit purposes.
  • You can update your name and WhatsApp number from the dashboard.

7. Security

  • Sign-in is via Google OAuth. We never see or store your Google password.
  • Your session uses a signed token stored in your browser only, valid for 7 days.
  • Administrative access to your record is limited to authorised operators and uses a separate short-lived (8-hour) session.

8. Retention

  • Active-account data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Payment references are retained after account deletion for financial audit and compliance.

9. Changes to these terms

We may update these terms as the service evolves. If we do, we will ask you to accept the updated version before you next continue.

10. Contact

WhatsApp support: +91 90198 79108.
